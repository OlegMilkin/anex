About Spectron IR - medical infrared imaging cameras worldwide

Spectron IR remains devoted to its customers to ensure the best value and quality possible. Spectron IR strives to be the leader in thermography for medical applications by constantly being on the leading edge of medical infrared imaging technology. Furthermore, when attending our free online demonstration, customers will be impressed with the quality, features and analytics provided by the software. CALL NOW to reserve your time slot!! 1-855-482-6444. Click here to view our software page. 

Spectron IR considers medical thermal imaging to be the adjunctive imaging technology choice of the future! Most importantly, thermography emits NO radiation, uses no consumables, and works in synergy with many of today’s common diagnostic procedures. In addition, thermography allows clinicians to provide their patients with a unique adjunctive functional evaluation.

Spectron IR includes the newest hardware and software technologies on the market with all its systems. This in turn allows clinicians to easily and adjunctively screen for: breast cancer, peripheral vascular disease, neuromusculoskeletal disorders, extracranial cerebral and facial vascular disease, thyroid gland abnormalities, and various other neoplastic, metabolic and inflammatory conditions.

It is Spectron IR’s goal to continually advance its technology base and provide excellent medical infrared imaging products to its customers worldwide!

Use of the Spectron IR is not intended to be a sole diagnostic procedure.
